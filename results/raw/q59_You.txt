It is one of the most commonly followed equity indices and includes approximately 80% of the total market capitalization of U.S. ... The S&P 500 index is a public float weighted/capitalization-weighted index. The ten largest companies on the S&P 500 index account for approximately 38% of the market capitalization of the index and the 50 largest components account for 60% of the index.
