# San Francisco Weather by Month

# January Weather in San Francisco

Kicking off the year, January weather in San Francisco is typically cool and wet. Expect a good chance of rain and chilly temperatures. Knowing the temperature in San Francisco in January will help you pack accordingly.

The average temperature in San Francisco in January ranges from a high of 13°C (55°F) to a low of 7°C (45°F). It's definitely a winter month! Pack layers, a waterproof jacket, and sturdy shoes.

What to wear and pack: Layers are your best friend! Pack a warm coat, sweaters, long-sleeved shirts, waterproof trousers, and comfortable walking shoes. Don't forget a scarf, hat, and gloves for those extra chilly days.

# February Weather in San Francisco

February weather in San Francisco often brings a mix of rain and sunshine, with a slight warming trend compared to January. Checking the February weather in San Francisco before you go is a must. The San Francisco weather February can be unpredictable.

The average temperature in San Francisco in February hovers between 14°C (57°F) and 8°C (46°F). It's still winter, but you might get glimpses of spring. Pack similar items as January, but maybe add a lighter jacket.

What to wear and pack: Similar to January, layers are key. A waterproof jacket is still essential, along with sweaters, long-sleeved shirts, and comfortable walking shoes. Consider packing a lighter jacket for those sunnier days.

# March Weather in San Francisco

As spring begins, March weather in San Francisco starts to show signs of improvement, though rain is still possible. Knowing the March weather in San Francisco will help you plan your activities. The San Francisco weather March can be quite variable.

The average temperature in San Francisco in March rises slightly, with highs around 16°C (61°F) and lows around 9°C (48°F). You might experience some lovely, sunny days. Pack layers, including a light jacket and a sweater.

What to wear and pack: Layers are still crucial. Pack a light jacket, sweaters, long-sleeved shirts, and comfortable walking shoes. A scarf and hat might still be useful, especially in the evenings.

# April Weather in San Francisco

April weather in San Francisco is often a mixed bag, with a blend of sunshine and the famous San Francisco fog. Checking the april weather in San Francisco is a good idea. The temperature in San Francisco in April is usually mild.

The average temperature in San Francisco in April is around 18°C (64°F) during the day, dropping to about 10°C (50°F) at night. Be prepared for both warm and cool conditions. Pack layers, including a light jacket and a sweater.

What to wear and pack: Pack layers, including a light jacket, sweaters, long-sleeved shirts, and comfortable walking shoes. Sunglasses are a good idea for those sunny days.

# San Francisco Seasons Weather

San Francisco experiences four distinct seasons, though the differences aren't as dramatic as in other parts of the world. The San Francisco climate is heavily influenced by the Pacific Ocean, leading to mild temperatures year-round.

Spring (March - May): The average temperature during spring is between 16°C to 19°C (61°F to 66°F). Spring in San Francisco is a time of blooming flowers and occasional rain.

Summer (June - August): The average temperature during summer is between 20°C to 21°C (68°F to 70°F). Summer in San Francisco is often foggy, especially in the mornings and evenings.

Autumn (September - November): The average temperature during autumn is between 17°C to 23°C (63°F to 73°F). Autumn is often the warmest and sunniest time of year in San Francisco.

Winter (December - February): The average temperature during winter is between 13°C to 14°C (55°F to 57°F). Winter in San Francisco is cool and wet, with a good chance of rain.

# Best Time to Visit San Francisco

The best time to visit San Francisco is generally during the "shoulder seasons" – September and October. The San Francisco weather is typically warm and sunny during these months, with less fog than in the summer. However, if you don't mind cooler temperatures and the possibility of fog, spring (April-May) and autumn (September-October) can also be great times to visit. The San Francisco weather now can be checked online.

Consider your priorities when planning your trip. If you want sunshine and warmth, aim for September or October. If you're on a budget, consider visiting during the off-season (winter), when prices for accommodation and flights are often lower.
