# Building and Creating Things for a Fulfilling Life

## How my lock-down maker hobbies made me a happier and more creative person

It’s no secret that people like to build and create things. Whether it’s for their work or a side project, both of them will usually consist of building something that they can share with people they care about.

The feeling of completing a project and having something that you’ve built from nothing is immensely satisfying and can lead to a more fulfilling life. It can make you feel alive and reveal talents and meanings in your life that you didn’t know you had.

During the lockdowns of 2020, I realized that I didn’t spend much time building and creating things. I made it my mission to use the time to try new hobbies and build new things and some of them stuck.

Here I am going to share some of the ones that stuck and some that I was doing long before any lockdown. Maybe you’ll resonate with some of them and will lead to you building something new.

## Building a Computer Setup

Press enter or click to view image in full size

![A custom PC build setup and desk](https://miro.medium.com/v2/resize:fit:700/0*062U8u2QsJOSnKxK)

Photo by [SCREEN POST](https://unsplash.com/@screenpost?utm_source=medium&utm_medium=referral) on [Unsplash](https://unsplash.com/?utm_source=medium&utm_medium=referral)

I’ve always been into computers and especially video games. Having a computer can lead to lots of creative outlets such as photo editing, video editing, 3d modeling, and digital drawing. I have dabbled in all of these but none of them would be possible without a computer. All of these are way more satisfying to me when they are created on a computer that I built and customized myself.

There are so many variations in parts and specs that the computer you build can become an expression of yourself. You can have RGB lighting, cases you can see through, different colored cables, water-cooling with custom colored liquid, and various types of keyboards and mice. The specs can be fine-tuned to what you want to use the computer for, whether it’s just browsing the web or playing graphically intense video games. Maybe you want three monitors or prefer a more minimalist setup with one monitor and one color of lighting instead of RGB lights flashing every second.
