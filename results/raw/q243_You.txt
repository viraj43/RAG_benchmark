Title: A Look Ahead at Clean Energy in 2025 | Department of Energy
Our support of DOE’s Energy Earthshots™ will help us focus our efforts to inspire economy-wide clean energy solutions, combat climate change, and bring affordable and reliable energy to all Americans. EERE’s technology offices spearhead six of the eight Energy Earthshots. EERE will prioritize continued innovation in emerging technologies that allow the U.S. to generate all electricity from clean, renewable sources. The Office of Energy Efficiency and Renewable Energy (EERE) highlights mission-critical investments to foster a 100% clean energy economy. EERE is more than a research and development funding vehicle, it is a nucleus of technology innovation and economy-wide cost-reduction and decarbonization efforts. EERE’s Renewable Energy Siting through Technical Engagement Planning (R-STEP) program is an example of this work in action, providing expertise and training to local governments and communities as they evaluate large-scale renewable energy and energy storage projects. EERE currently funds research and development of technologies and processes to help solve the riddle of industrial decarbonization. Once we solve this riddle and others like it, we can support manufacturing right here at home for the clean energy technologies and devices we need today.
