## What makes a great productivity tool for entrepreneurs?
The average entrepreneur wastes more than a third of their week on minor tasks like invoicing, data entry, and ordering office supplies.
Think about it:
If you’re making $100K a year, every hour is worth roughly $50. Those 13 wasted hours each week? That's $650 down the drain every single week.
Over a year, that's $33,800 in lost productivity. Not to mention all the stress, burnout, and mental health concerns that come from overworking.
Now imagine if you could reclaim just half of those hours and reinvest them into revenue-generating activities.
That would be huge for your business!
We (my wife Carey and I) built a multi-seven-figure business working just 20 hours a week – and we’ve since helped 50,000+ other entrepreneurs improve how much they get done. Thanks to our systems, we’ve been able to travel the world, live abroad, and spend more time with our three daughters.
And the right productivity tools can make a big difference.
That said, 95% of time management tools out there aren’t effective – especially if you don’t use them in the right way.
Instead, the best tools include:
Fast setup (if it takes more than 15 minutes to get value, it's not worth it)
Intuitive design (so that they’re incredibly easy to understand and use)
Automation capabilities (if you're still doing manual work, you're doing it wrong)
A good ROI (the benefits should be obvious within 30 days)
Seamless integration (plays nice with tools you already use)
