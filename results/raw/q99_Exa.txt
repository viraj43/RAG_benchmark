Title: Zuni Café - San Francisco
Key Highlights:
Zuni Café opened in San Francisco, California on February 15, 1979. Zuni Café won the James Beard Foundation Award for ‘Outstanding Restaurant’ in 2003 and ‘Outstanding Service’ in 2018.
[...]
We are located at 1658 Market Street, on the north side, between Franklin and Gough Streets. We are within walking distance of the San Francisco Jazz Center, Opera House, Davies Symphony Hall, The Orpheum Theater, and San Francisco City Hall. The closest BART station is Civic Center and the closest underground MUNI station is Van Ness.
