# 7 Best Coworking Spaces in San Francisco

By

[Chloe Saraceni](https://www.7x7.com/u/chloesaraceni) and [Shoshi Parks](https://www.7x7.com/u/shoshiparks)

Sep 14, 2023

### Still stuck in pandemic-born work-from-home limbo? Maxxing out time at your local coffee shop just to get a little human interaction? There is a better way.

What you need is the pro design of the office with the freedom of home and the coffee and snacks of said cafe. Although Covid caused a fair number of SF's coworking spaces to close their doors, there's still more out there than just WeWork.

* * *

Whether you're new to venturing beyond your home office or a pre-pandemic shared office afficionado ready for a return to the outside world, our favorite coworking spots in the city will help make your job a little more enjoyable.

![](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%201080%20719'%3E%3C/svg%3E)(Courtesy of [@ecosystemcoworkingsf](https://www.instagram.com/ecosystemcoworkingsf))

**Eco-System**

Eco-System is a seamless, multi-functional hub for creatives from all walks of life. In partnership with the Mirus Gallery and Temple Nightclub, members are treated to an airy, earth-toned communal workspace with a rotating display of fine art—plus, a fully-stocked kitchen with complementary coffee. _// Memberships offer 24/7 access plus free entry to Temple (contact Eco-System for rates); 540 Howard St., 2nd Floor (SoMa),_ [_ecosystemcoworkingsf.com_](https://ecosystemcoworkingsf.com/)

**Workshop Cafe**

Part coffee shop, part coworking space, the Workshop Cafe is a membership-free option that harnesses the power of creativity. Drop in to their FiDi or SoMa locations and pay by the hour for access to a communal space complete with printers, scanners, and plenty of outlets or for rentable meeting rooms and event spaces of various sizes. The onsite cafe will keep you plied with coffee and food throughout the day. // _Rates start at $3.50/hr; 180 Montgomery St. (FiDi) or 25 Spear St. (SoMa),_ _workshopcafe.com_

![](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%20980%20654'%3E%3C/svg%3E)(Courtesy of [@canopy.space](https://www.instagram.com/canopy.space))

**Canopy**

Fans of industrial design star Yves Béhar will happily settle into their Herman Miller chairs at Canopy, a vibrant, modern coworking space with Jackson Square and Pac Heights locations, as well as a multilevel FiDi outpost. There's a seriousness in the design here not seen at the others on this list, not to mention stylish brand partnerships. In other words, you may find yourself working among the particular friends of Béhar and the art-and tech-world elite. _// Memberships start at $365/month for a hot desk in shared space for 10 days per month and range all the way up to $2,900 for a private office, and offer 24/7 access; 2193 Fillmore St. (Pacific Heights), 595 Pacific Ave. (Jackson Square), 353 Kearny St. (FiDi),_ _[canopy.space](https://canopy.space/)_

![](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%20900%20600'%3E%3C/svg%3E)(Courtesy of [@trellisworkspace](https://www.instagram.com/trellisworkspace))

**Trellis**

The airy, industrial Trellis is a flexible space for both occasional and daily work. You can even pop in for just an hour at a time for just $5. In addition to bookable meeting rooms you can elevate your productivity with artisinal coffee (there's a barista on site), fresh pastires, and drink from the wine bar. // _Monthly memberships start at $275, day passes $28; 981 Mission St. (SoMa),_ [trellis.social](https://www.trellis.social/)

![](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%20900%20600'%3E%3C/svg%3E)(Courtesy of [@avant.space](https://www.instagram.com/avant.space/))

**AvantSpace**

AvantSpace blends San Francisco's high-tech atmosphere with upscale, boutique amenities in a warm, indoor/outdoor space equipped with high-speed WiFi, concierge services, and bottomless Blue Bottle Coffee in the fully tricked out kitchen—plus a slew of cultural and networking events. _// Memberships (starting at $275/month for 5 drop-in days or $550/month for a solo floating desk) includes 24/7 access; 2181 Greenwich St. (Cow Hollow), and 835 Fifth Ave. (San Rafael),_ _[avant.space.com](https://avant.space/)_

**Mindspace**

This massive Market Street space spans three floors of downtown real estate. Bright and modern on the inside, public transportation friendly on the outside, Mindspace also hosts professional events and weekly yoga. The boutique workspace has sister locations around Europe and the East Coast if you need a place to get things done while on the road. // _$400/month hot desk; 575 Market St. (Financial District),_ [_mindspace.me_](https://www.mindspace.me/l/market-st/)

![](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%20900%20599'%3E%3C/svg%3E)(Courtesy of [@wework](https://www.instagram.com/wework/))

**WeWork**

WeWork is basically synonymous with coworking no matter where you live—with locations around the world, you can think of it as the Uber of office space. SF currently has seven WeWork locations, each offering shared and dedicated desk spaces, dog-friendly lounges, and full kitchens with complimentary coffee from a rotating selection of local vendors. _// Day passes start at $29/day, contact WeWork for monthly membership rates which include 24/7 access; multiple locations,_ _wework.com_

### Why choose San Francisco for shared office space?

San Francisco’s coworking scene reflects the city’s innovative energy, offering flexible, thoughtfully designed workspaces for every type of professional. Whether you’re building a product, taking client calls, or collaborating with a team, coworking spaces across the city provide productive environments with modern layouts and strong community focus.

Each neighborhood brings a distinct feel. SoMa attracts tech professionals who value open floor plans and transit access. The Mission features creative, culture-driven spaces filled with character. Downtown and the Financial District offer polished offices with skyline views and a business-forward atmosphere. Dogpatch, Hayes Valley, and Potrero Hill appeal to founders and freelancers seeking light-filled, boutique-style settings.

Options include hot desks for flexibility and dedicated desks for consistency. Most spaces offer high-speed Wi-Fi, ergonomic seating, private phone booths, meeting areas, and comfortable lounges.

With Deskpass, you can browse coworking spaces across San Francisco, book day passes instantly, and choose the workspace that keeps you productive and connected.

## FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Can Members use other CANOPY locations?

Yes. All CANOPY members are welcome to work from any of our San Francisco Bay Area coworking spaces.

Is it easy to find available conference rooms or phone booths when I need them?

Yes. All CANOPY locations are designed with soundproofed phone booths and ample conference rooms for up to 14 people to ensure you can always find a private space when you need it—whether it’s for an impromptu call or a scheduled client meeting.

Do you offer networking events at CANOPY locations?

Yes. CANOPY has a robust event calendar that includes monthly gatherings exclusively for our members at all locations. These events provide meaningful opportunities to connect with fellow members and build relationships across our broader CANOPY community. In addition to Member mixers, our calendar features thought-provoking programs and panels centered around CANOPY’s core event pillars—health and wellness, technology, venture capital, AI, art and design, climate and sustainability, startups, and science.

How does design play a role in the CANOPY experience?

Design informs every aspect of CANOPY’s SF shared office spaces. From acoustics to layout to finishes, every element is intentionally crafted to create inspiring, high-performance spaces. A thoughtful balance of workspace types—from private offices to communal tables and lounges—ensures the right setting for both focus and collaboration.

What types of workspaces does each CANOPY location offer?

EveryCANOPY shared office space is designed with elegance, functionality, and focus in mind.

At each location, you can choose from:

- **Private Offices** of various sizes with natural light and ergonomic furniture.
- **Dedicated Personal Desks** in thoughtfully arranged shared environments.
- **Communal Work Lounges** with designer furniture and quiet, respectful energy.
- **Conference Rooms & Boardrooms** equipped with top-tier AV and presentation tools.
- **Phone Booths** for private calls.
- **Outdoor Terraces** and curated common areas for informal meetings or creative thinking.

All of our spaces are meticulously designed to inspire productivity and promote well-being, blending hospitality, design, and performance in every detail and allowing for private, semi-private, or open spaces for collaboration and bursts of innovation.

What types of Memberships does CANOPY offer?

CANOPY offers a curated range of Memberships designed to support every stage of your work life, whether you need a dedicated space, a vibrant communal environment, or something more flexible. Each Membership is named for the type of access you’ll enjoy.

- **Private Office:** Fully enclosed, furnished offices ideal for individuals or teams seeking privacy, focus, and premium amenities.
- **Personal Desk:** Your own dedicated desk within our shared workspace — perfect for those who thrive in a consistent, elevated environment.
- **Full-Time Communal Membership:** Unlimited access to our beautifully designed communal workspaces, ideal for professionals who want a full-time presence without the need for a dedicated space.
- **Hybrid Membership:** Flexible access to our shared workspace for the occasional user — a seamless solution for those splitting time between home and the office.
- **Virtual Office:** CANOPY can be the virtual home of any business, offering a ready-made address and mail service including a virtual mailbox, mail scanning, mail forwarding, and letter shredding.

Each Membership includes access to high-speed internet, phone booths, beautifully designed common areas, and thoughtfully curated member perks.

Can I host events at CANOPY?

Absolutely. CANOPY’s flexible venues are ideal for hosting a corporate offsite, product launch, networking reception, or celebratory dinner. Each location features thoughtfully curated interiors, premium amenities, and dedicated on-site events teams to ensure a seamless experience from start to finish. Whether you’re planning a large gathering or an intimate workshop, our meeting and event spaces can be tailored to fit your needs. Catering, A/V setup, and concierge services are also available upon request.
