First, State-by-State regulation by definition creates a patchwork of 50 different regulatory regimes that makes compliance more challenging, particularly for start-ups. The US artificial intelligence (AI) regulatory landscape is at an inflection point. With accelerating pace starting in the 2020s, hundreds of proposed state measures signaled a fast-developing state-level regulatory AI landscape.
