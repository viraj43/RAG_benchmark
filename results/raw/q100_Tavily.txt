# Things to Do in San Francisco This Weekend (When You Have No Plans)

CityDays’ guide to finding things to do in San Francisco this weekend when you have no plans (or you've run out of ideas).

Table of Contents

Looking for the best **things to do in San Francisco this weekend**? You’re in the right place!

San Francisco is bursting with excitement, from iconic landmarks and hidden gems to outdoor adventures and cultural hotspots.

![things to do in San Francisco this weekend 02](/blog-assets/media/images/things_to_do_in_San_Francisco_this_weekend_02.width-800.jpg)

Whether you’re a local craving a fresh weekend plan or a visitor ready to explore the city’s charm, this guide has something for everyone. Discover scenic hikes, unravel urban mysteries, or dive into the city’s rich history and thriving art scene.

Whatever your vibe, these handpicked activities will make your weekend in San Francisco unforgettable. Get ready to make the most of this amazing city!

***And, as always, everything here has been chosen by us and recommended by us. Nobody pays us to be featured on our website. No affiliate links, no advertising, just places we’ve been or experiences we’ve loved so we can show you the*** [***best things to do in San Francisco***](https://citydays.com/things-to-do/san-francisco/)***.***

## The Best Things to Do in San Francisco This Weekend

![](/blog-assets/media/images/things_to_do_in_San_Francisco_with.2e16d0ba.fill-720x450_0aFoPpP_lazy.jpg)

[## 1 - Go on a Scavenger Hunt Around San Francisco](https://citydays.com/san-francisco-scavenger-hunts/)

Looking for things to do in San Francisco this weekend that get you out of the house?

Unleash your inner detective with a city-wide scavenger hunt! Explore San Francisco’s nooks and crannies while solving clever puzzles and uncovering hidden gems. Perfect for getting in your step count and showing off how smart you are!

![](/blog-assets/media/images/things_to_do_in_San_Francisco_this.2e16d0ba.fill-720x450_lazy.jpg)

[## 2 - Sing Along at a Broadway Show](https://www.broadwaysf.com/)

Now that *Wicked* has hit the movie theaters as well as the big stage, I know I'm not the only one who’s ready for more show-stopping numbers and toe-tapping tunes!

San Francisco’s Broadway scene is alive with world-class performances that will have you singing all the way home. Find out what’s playing and grab some leftover tickets for a weekend full of the right kind of drama!

![](/blog-assets/media/images/things_to_do_in_San_Francisco_with.2e16d0ba.fill-720x450_Hb4NYlQ_lazy.jpg)

[## 3 - Find a Secret Garden in an Urban Exploration Game](https://citydays.com/san-francisco-exploration-games/)

As the saying goes, we don’t stop dancing because we get old, we get old because we stop dancing. Except, trade dancing for exploring.

If you’re looking for things to do in San Francisco this weekend, grab some friends, your family or your partner and watch as the city gets turned into your playground!

Follow cryptic clues that lead you to San Francisco’s lesser-known wonders, including hidden gardens and scenic spots, and be prepared to share local trivia with everyone you know for the next few weeks.

![](/blog-assets/media/images/things_to_do_in_San_Francisco_this.2e16d0ba.fill-720x450_7CcmcSG_lazy.jpg)

[## 4 - Take in The View From Christmas Tree Point](https://citydays.com/places/christmas-tree-point/)

For unbeatable panoramic views of San Francisco, head to Christmas Tree Point.

Since the holiday lights have been put up and San Francisco is now at its “glowiest”, this iconic spot atop Twin Peaks offers a spectacular vantage point for getting into the holiday spirit.

Bring your camera, and prepare to be wowed!

[## 5 - Check Out The Conservatory of Flowers](https://citydays.com/places/conservatory-of-flowers/)

Winter, summer, fall or spring, it’s always flower time over at the Conservatory of Flowers.

This Victorian gem houses a vibrant collection of tropical plants and exotic blooms, providing a serene escape from the hustle and bustle of the city and wintery temps.

Stroll through lush greenery, marvel at carnivorous plants, and snap photos of vibrant blooms–your Instagram feed will thank you for it.

![](/blog-assets/media/images/Sutro_Baths_.2e16d0ba.fill-720x450_lazy.jpg)

[## 6 - See The Sutro Baths Ruins](https://citydays.com/places/sutro-baths/)

If you’re looking for things to do in San Francisco this weekend that involve a little time travel without the hassle of building your own machine, you could always channel your inner archaeologist at the Sutro Baths ruins.

Explore the remains of what was once a luxurious bathhouse and imagine its former grandeur as you take an adventurous stroll or grab some Instagram-worthy shots.

[## 7 - Go Ice Skating at Union Square](https://unionsquareicerink.com/)

Did you know that there was a plan to turn Sutro Baths into an ice rink but it ultimately failed? Well, it doesn’t matter, because you can still get your skates on this weekend (not at the baths, though).

Lace up your skates and glide into the holiday spirit at Union Square’s iconic ice rink. Even if you’re more of a “clinging to the wall” type, it’s a delightful way to embrace the season.

![](/blog-assets/media/images/things_to_do_in_San_Francisco_this.2e16d0ba.fill-720x450_70PMDjQ_lazy.jpg)

[## 8 - Look Around The Ferry Building Farmers Market](https://www.ferrybuildingmarketplace.com/farmers-market/)

Foodies, this one’s for you! The Ferry Building Farmers Market is a feast of flavors, offering everything from fresh produce to artisanal cheeses and handmade chocolates. Sample local delicacies, chat with friendly vendors, and stock up on unique treats you won’t find anywhere else.

Come hungry and leave with a bag (or two) of goodies.

[## 9 - Hike Around Muir Woods](https://www.nps.gov/muwo/index.htm)

Looking for things to do in San Francisco this weekend that come with a dose of fresh air?

Trade skyscrapers for Redwoods at Muir Woods. Wander along shaded trails, breathe in the crisp, earthy air, and feel your worries melt away beneath the canopy of these majestic giants.

For a little adventure, tackle a scenic loop or climb to higher viewpoints for jaw-dropping panoramas.

![](/blog-assets/media/images/things_to_do_in_San_Francisco_this.2e16d0ba.fill-720x450_jVDKBSK_lazy.jpg)

[## 10 - See What’s On at SFMOMA](https://www.sfmoma.org/)

It’s been a minute since you last visited (or maybe you’ve never been before!) but this is your sign to immerse yourself in the world of modern art at SFMOMA.

With innovative exhibits and striking works from renowned artists, it’s a cultural experience that’s anything but ordinary. From mind-bending contemporary installations to thought-provoking photography and classic works by artists like Frida Kahlo and Andy Warhol, there’s always something to spark your imagination.

Don’t miss the rooftop garden for a breath of fresh air!

![](/blog-assets/media/images/Birthday_ideas_in_San_Francisco_09.2e16d0ba.fill-720x450_lazy.jpg)

[## 11 - Discover San Francisco’s History on an Urban Adventure](https://citydays.com/san-francisco-urban-adventures/)

History buffs and thrill-seekers, unite! Urban adventures like these let you peel back the layers of San Francisco’s rich history in the most interactive way possible.

Solve riddles, uncover secrets, and explore the city’s landmarks while learning quirky stories about its past…

## 12 - Visit Festive Holiday Bars

'Tis the season to drink and be merry and you know what that means: it’s holiday bar time.

San Francisco’s holiday-themed bars are serving up festive cheer by the glass, and as this is the prime time to visit them, it’d almost be rude not to sip on creative cocktails in over-the-top decor that would make even the Grinch smile.

![](/blog-assets/media/images/things_to_do_in_San_Francisco_for_.2e16d0ba.fill-720x450_lazy.jpg)

[## 13 - Check Out The Exhibits at Alcatraz](https://www.nps.gov/alca/index.htm)

Take a ferry ride to the infamous Alcatraz Island and uncover the secrets of “The Rock.”

Once home to infamous inmates like Al Capone, this former prison now offers fascinating exhibits that bring its history to life. Wander through eerie cell blocks, explore the prison yard, and imagine the daring escape attempts that became the stuff of legend.

![](/blog-assets/media/images/things_to_do_in_san_francisco_this.2e16d0ba.fill-720x450_lazy.jpg)

[## 14 - See The Cable Car Museum](http://www.cablecarmuseum.org/)

Hop off a cable car and step into history at the Cable Car Museum!

Marvel at the massive machinery that keeps the cables running, explore vintage cars up close, and dive into the stories of the people who built and operated them. Admission is free, making it a fun and budget-friendly way to experience a piece of living history.

Pro tip: The working powerhouse beneath the museum will leave you as impressed as the views you just rode in on!

[## 15 - Get Some Fresh Air at San Francisco Botanical Garden](https://gggp.org/san-francisco-botanical-garden/)

Looking for things to do in San Francisco this weekend that give you a break from the stress of the holidays?

Take a deep breath and let nature work its magic at the San Francisco Botanical Garden.

Wander through 55 acres of lush landscapes showcasing plants from around the globe, from towering redwoods to vibrant tropical blooms.

![](/blog-assets/media/images/things_to_do_in_San_Francisco_this.2e16d0ba.fill-720x450_2hviWUz_lazy.jpg)

## 16 - Climb The Many Steps of SF For The Best Views in The City

Get your heart pumping and your camera clicking as you tackle San Francisco’s famous staircases. [The Filbert Street Steps](https://citydays.com/places/filbert-street-steps/) wind through lush greenery and charming gardens, while the [Lyon Street Steps](https://citydays.com/places/lyon-street-steps/) reward your efforts with jaw-dropping views of the Golden Gate Bridge. For a splash of color, head to the [16th Avenue Tiled Steps](https://citydays.com/places/16th-avenue-tiled-steps/), where intricate mosaics guide you skyward.

And if you’re not in the mood for climbing today…why not whizz down the [Seward Street Slides](https://citydays.com/places/the-seward-street-slides/) instead.

# 30 Memorable Ways To Make The Most Of May 2026 In San Francisco

Happy May, San Francisco! From [Bay to Breakers](https://secretsanfrancisco.com/bay-to-breakers-sf/) to free museum days, there are plenty of spring events and activities to check out this month in the Bay Area.

We can’t wait to enjoy these activities, events, and seasonal **things to do this May 2026 in San Francisco.** Enjoy the peak of the [spring season](https://secretsanfrancisco.com/spring-bucket-list-sf/) with a lively afternoon at Bay to Breakers, a sunny stroll through blooming flower fields, and a massive party over Memorial Day weekend at Carnaval SF.

Whether you’re a foodie planning ahead for the Embarcadero Center Wine Walk or you want to get the best deals for a museum visit, we’ve got all the details.  **Scroll down to start planning your May in SF!**

Downtown First Thursdays is a monthly block party featuring over 175 **vendors, performers, and activities** on 2nd Street. The enormous free events attract thousands of San Franciscans for a memorable and cost-effective night out in the city.

## [AURA at Grace Cathedral](https://feverup.com/m/434520)

![Light fills a stunning venue at AURA](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%201200%20800'%3E%3C/svg%3E)
![Light fills a stunning venue at AURA](https://offloadmedia.feverup.com/secretsanfrancisco.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/09/02190117/3-1024x683.jpg)

Grace Cathedral

AURA at Grace Cathedral is a **45-minute immersive show** that blends music, visual projections, and the cathedral’s breathtaking architecture. Celebrated around the world, the experience turns the historic space into a **dynamic art installation,** illuminating its arches and intricate details through light and sound.
