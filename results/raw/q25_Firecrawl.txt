# AI Startups in San Francisco to Follow in 2026

Mar 9, 2026

## **AI Innovation Takes Root in San Francisco**

San Francisco startups help healthcare providers improve patient outcomes, manufacturers modernize quality control, SMBs access capital, and enterprises operationalize AI at scale. Powered by deep technical talent and enterprise demand, the city continues to lead in applied AI innovation.

Below are San Francisco-based AI companies to watch in 2026.

### **AiStrike**

**AiStrike helps organizations strengthen cyber defense proactively** through an AI-native, agentic security operations platform. The company enables security teams to move beyond reactive SOC and MDR models by continuously improving detection coverage, proactively hunting threats, and reducing risk across distributed environments.

Delivered via its AI-native platform and Agentic Cyber Defense as a Service model, AiStrike operates, tunes, and optimizes AI-driven defenses so customers can focus on security outcomes rather than manual operations.

### **Overview AI**

[**Overview AI**](https://blumbergcapital.com/portfolio-companies/overview/) **helps manufacturers automate inspection and modernize quality control** with deep learning–powered vision systems and an integrated workflow platform. Its AI-first architecture adapts to dynamic production environments, enabling teams to reduce inspection costs, lower rework, and improve defect detection in real-world manufacturing settings.

### **Angle Health**

[**Angle Health**](https://blumbergcapital.com/portfolio-companies/angle-health/) **helps employers provide transparent, affordable healthcare benefits** through an AI-powered platform that combines underwriting, claims administration, and member engagement. Businesses of all sizes use Angle to offer customized benefits plans and personalized healthcare experiences.

Through its Benefit Builder and Quote-to-Card platforms, Angle enables brokers to generate firm underwritten quotes in minutes using census data and implement groups in real time.

### **Streamline AI**

**Streamline AI helps in-house legal teams automate intake, triage, and matter management** through an AI-powered workflow platform. By centralizing requests, workflows, and performance metrics, the platform enables legal departments to operate more efficiently and transparently.

### **Kloudfuse**

[**Kloudfuse**](https://blumbergcapital.com/portfolio-companies/kloudfuse/) **helps engineering teams gain unified visibility across infrastructure and applications** through a consolidated observability platform. By integrating monitoring, logging, and performance tracking within a single data layer, the platform enables faster troubleshooting, stronger security, and improved cost control.

### **Segmed**

[**Segmed**](https://blumbergcapital.com/portfolio-companies/segmed/) **helps life sciences and healthcare organizations accelerate research and AI development** by providing streamlined access to high-quality, de-identified medical imaging data. Through a global network of partners, the company delivers access to more than 100 million standardized imaging datasets via its proprietary platform.

### **Fundbox**

[**Fundbox**](https://blumbergcapital.com/portfolio-companies/fundbox/) **helps small and midsize businesses access capital directly within the tools they already use** through embedded finance infrastructure. By integrating credit products into software platforms, Fundbox enables SMBs to unlock working capital quickly and seamlessly.

### **Nexla**

[**Nexla**](https://blumbergcapital.com/portfolio-companies/nexla/) **helps enterprises make data AI-ready and usable at scale** with an AI-powered integration platform. Organizations use Nexla to connect disparate data sources, create reusable data products, and accelerate analytics and generative AI initiatives without extensive coding.

### **Joshu**

[**Joshu**](https://blumbergcapital.com/portfolio-companies/joshu/) **helps insurers launch digital distribution channels faster and with less IT dependency** through a configurable product development and portal platform. Insurance professionals use Joshu to design products, deploy user-friendly experiences, and accelerate go-to-market timelines.

### **Ferrum Health**

[**Ferrum Health**](https://blumbergcapital.com/portfolio-companies/ferrum-health/) **helps health systems deploy and scale clinical AI solutions** through an enterprise AI platform that integrates imaging and administrative data across service lines. Its end-to-end AI hubs allow hospitals to run multiple AI applications in a secure, governed environment. To date, more than 2.5 million unique patient records have been analyzed using the platform.

>
> “Every major technology wave starts in San Francisco, and AI is no different. The density of talent and capital here continues to be a real advantage—even as companies scale globally and build teams closer to their customers.” – Jared Katzen, vice president, Blumberg Capital

### **The Future of AI in San Francisco**

San Francisco startups help enterprises move from experimentation to operational AI adoption. As artificial intelligence becomes foundational infrastructure across industries, companies emerging from the region continue to shape how AI integrates into healthcare, finance, commerce, and enterprise systems worldwide.
