Title: The Best Day Trips from San Francisco (Updated for 2026)
Key Highlights:
Listing my favorite places near San Francisco and what I reckon to be the best day trips from San Francisco!
[...]
In this blog, I list 12 of the best day trips from San Francisco. I’ve included some super-close towns like the lovely surf haven of Half Moon Bay, just a 40-minute drive from the city. And the coastal town of Tiburon, which is just a 30-minute drive away. But I’ve also included some farther-flung, must-visit places like Yosemite National Park and Carmel-by-the-Sea. Two of my favorite places to visit in all of California.
[...]
So, let’s explore 12 of the not-to-be-missed day trips from San Francisco. There’s something for everyone on this list!
[...]
The best day trips close to San Francisco include Sausalito (a 20-minute drive away) – which is a laidback town with a Mediterranean feel that’s most famous for its houseboats and scenic views. Mill Valley and Muir Woods are also close to downtown at around a 30-minute drive each way. Mill Valley is like something from a storybook, and Muir Woods is one of the most popular tourist attractions in the state – it’s home to giant redwood trees.

# San Francisco to Bodega Bay Day Trip
[...]
Experience Northern California’s dramatic coastline on a visit to Bodega Bay. Just over an hour’s drive from San Francisco’s Golden Gate Bridge through the rolling hills and pasturelands of Sonoma County’s lush farm belt, Bodega Bay is a seaside village in scenic Sonoma Wine Country.
[...]
Whether you relax on a sandy beach, explore tide pools, hike bluff-top trails, savor premium wine and cuisine, or enjoy the amazing views along the famous highway, here is your handy guide to a memorable day in Bodega Bay.
[...]
From the Golden Gate Bridge, cruise Highway 101 toward the charming town of Petaluma. As you head towards the coast, buildings and homes gradually give way to weathered barns and sheds, neat farmhouses, gentle hillsides, and pastures with cattle, sheep, goats, and horses. Enjoy the views around each bend.
[...]
If you have the time, stop in tiny Valley Ford(pop. 147). Discover a small collection of popular restaurants and shops, as well as the historic Valley Ford Hotel(built in 1864), and the family-owned Valley Ford Market, a farmers’ and ranchers’ grocery store with plenty to offer visitors.

This small community offers a delightful mix of shops, restaurants, art galleries, and wonderful ways to explore the stunning natural beauty. Stop at Candy & Kites for a colorful kite or two before you hit the beach.
[...]
Start by getting your feet in the sand at Doran Regional Park, a perfect spot for beachcombing, picnicking, fishing, building a sand castle, or sitting back to enjoy the day. Scramble over the rock jetty at the harbor mouth and explore pools of sea life.
[...]
To explore the natural wonders of this area, head to Bodega Head, a rocky peninsula that shelters the town of Bodega Bay from the mighty Pacific Ocean. Here you’ll find trails that offer dazzling ocean and bay views, as well as access to small beaches.

Explore the natural wonders of Bodega Bay firsthand. Nothing beats touring this rugged coastal beauty like a horseback riding experience. Five Brooks at Bodega Bay and Horse N Around Trail Rides offer a variety of guided trail rides for every experience level to see breathtaking views along the sand dunes, hills, or beach.
[...]
If the water is calling, Bodega Bay Kayak rents kayaks and stand-up paddleboards and offers lessons and customized guided tours on the ocean, bay, river, or estuary. Rentals include all equipment, wetsuit, life vest, and safety instruction before heading out.

# Day Trip From San Francisco to Healdsburg
[...]
Discover California’s famous Sonoma Wine Country, including fine wine, farm-to-table cuisine, art, breathtaking scenery, and small-town charm when you take an easy excursion into Healdsburg during your time in San Francisco.
[...]
Trendy Healdsburg(pop. 11,541) is a food and wine oasis where you can savor the good life and soak up the vineyard countryside, about a 90-minute drive north of the Golden Gate Bridge. Here is a handy guide to enjoy the day in Healdsburg in the heart of Sonoma Wine Country.
[...]
Healdsburg’s historic central plaza was named by Travel + Leisure Magazine as one of “America’s Most Beautiful Town Squares.” Indeed, the charming outdoor bandstand here hosts free concerts, antique fairs, and festivities.
[...]
Start the day right at Costeaux French Bakery. This inviting spot has been a Healdsburg institution since 1923 and is celebrated for its artisan bread, fresh pastries, and delicious breakfasts.
[...]
Around Healdsburg’s pretty Plaza, you’ll find everything from antiques and fine art to clothing, books, and farm-fresh delights. For art lovers, some two dozen Healdsburg art galleries are within walking distance of the Plaza.
[...]
The 4,000-square-foot Modern Antiquarium overflows with treasures from more than 30 antique vendors, just a few blocks off the Plaza.
[...]
You can walk to more than two dozen winery tasting rooms in Healdsburg, and strolling is an excellent way to sip, savor, and shop for extraordinary wines in the heart of Sonoma Wine Country.
[...]
To get an inside view of the notable food, wine, and rich history of this renowned region, take a memorable (and delicious!) walking tour with these favorite guides:

Take a scenic cruise to visit the area’s gorgeous vineyard estates in the surrounding wine appellations of the Alexander Valley, Dry Creek Valley, and Russian River Valley.
[...]
Stretch your legs on self-guided free vineyard walking tours in Sonoma Wine Country, such as the memorable meander through five acres of dazzling garden-scapes at Ferrari-Carano Vineyards and Winery. Another fun trail is at the fascinating Francis Ford Coppola Winery in nearby Geyserville.

The Healdsburg area offers gorgeous cycling routes through acres of vineyards, stunning vistas, and quiet roadways. Ace It Bike Tours offers a Healdsburg Wine Country Cycling tour, and Getaway Adventures offers a Healdsburg Sip ‘N Cycle tour.

Sip wine before dinner at tasting rooms on the Healdsburg Plaza, which are open later, such as the inviting space at Thumbprint Cellars.

# 25. Healdsburg
[...]
Healdsburg is a culinary and wine hotspot in Northern Sonoma County, with a variety of farm-to-table restaurants showcasing local and seasonal foods. A lovely walkable downtown, spas, and a wide range of activities in and around town make for a fantastic day trip from San Francisco.
